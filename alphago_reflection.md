**What moment in the film impacted you the most, and why?**

How fast computers can learn to play a game like Go, and how quickly they can surpass human players. It was fascinating to see how AlphaGo was able to learn from its mistakes and improve its gameplay over time.

**How did AlphaGo’s approach to decision-making differ from that of its human opponents?**

It relied on a big database of past games and used deep learning to analyze patterns and make decisions. It also had the ability to simulate many possible moves and outcomes, allowing it to make more informed choices than its human opponents.

**What surprised you about how AlphaGo learned or played the game?**

It played each move relatively quickly, and it was able to make moves that were unexpected and unconventional. It was also surprising how quickly it was able to learn from its mistakes and improve its gameplay.

**Do you believe AlphaGo demonstrated creativity? Defend your answer.**

Not really, since even though it was able to make unconventional moves, it was still relying on a database of past games and patterns. It was not truly creating new strategies or ideas, but rather using existing knowledge in a novel way.
